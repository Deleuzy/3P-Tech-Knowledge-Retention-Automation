## Best Practices
- All EX equipment must carry a valid certificate (IECEx or ATEX) per DS/EN 60079-14 §6.1.
- Cable glands must be EX-certified and correctly torqued to IP rating per DS/EN 60079-14 §9.3.
- Junction boxes in Zone 1 must be EX d or EX e rated and fully closed per DS/EN 60079-14 §10.4.
- PE conductor cross-section must be ≥50mm² for lightning protection bonding per DS/EN 60079-14 §12.2.
- All instrument cables in Zone 1 must be shielded, with a minimum cross-section of 0.75mm² per DS/EN 60079-14 §6.3.3.
- Lightning protection system inspection and bonding verification required per DS/EN 62305-3.

## Kendte Problemer og Afvigelser
- Damaged EX d cable glands on NH3 instruments in Zone 1 - requires replacement.
- Junction box cover loose on 80HLS002 in Zone 1 - requires tightening and re-certification.
- Missing PE conductor on NH3 panel earth bar - requires installation.
- Lightning protection bonding insufficient - requires upgrade to 50mm².

## Tjekliste
1. Verify certificates for all EX equipment.
2. Replace all damaged EX d cable glands on NH3 instruments.
3. Tighten and re-certify junction box cover on 80HLS002.
4. Install missing PE conductor on NH3 panel earth bar.
5. Upgrade lightning protection bonding conductor to 50mm².
6. Obtain and file EX certificates for 80PT004 and 80HLS002.
7. Commission full lightning protection survey (DS/EN 62305-3).
8. Set up periodic inspection schedule per DS/EN 60079-17.

## Lessons Learned
- Conduct thorough inspections and document findings.
- Address any deviations or non-conformities promptly.
- Ensure compliance with relevant standards and regulations.
