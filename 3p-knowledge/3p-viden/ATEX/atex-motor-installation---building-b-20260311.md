## Lessons Learned

**ATEX Motor Installation - Building B**

- **Root Cause**: Root Cause: The original motor specification (Ex d) was changed late in the project to Ex e (increased safety) due to a misinterpretation of the zone classification. This required re-wiring and additional certification work.

Cost: Extra 75,000 DKK in material costs and 40 hours of additional labor (32,000 DKK). Total impact: 107,000 DKK.

- **What Happened**: 1. Verify zone classification with the ATEX coordinator BEFORE finalizing equipment specifications
2. Always get a second review of zone 1/2 equipment selections
3. Include ATEX certification costs in initial budget estimates
