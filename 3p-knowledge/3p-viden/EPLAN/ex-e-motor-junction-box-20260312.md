## Lessons Learned

**Ex e Motor Junction Box**

- **Root Cause**: Increased safety motor (Ex e) junction box used for Zone 1 instead of Ex d. Certification mismatch discovered during inspection. Replacement cost 42000 DKK.
- **What Happened**: Verify enclosure type matches zone classification. Ex e is for Zone 2, Ex d is required for Zone 1.
- **Cost**: cost 42000 DKK
