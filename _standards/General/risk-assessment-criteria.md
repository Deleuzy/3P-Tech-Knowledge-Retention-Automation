# Risk Assessment Criteria — 3P Teknik Standard Reference

## Risk Matrix

| Severity \ Likelihood | Unlikely | Possible | Likely |
|---|---|---|---|
| Minor | LOW | LOW | MEDIUM |
| Moderate | LOW | MEDIUM | HIGH |
| Critical | MEDIUM | HIGH | CRITICAL |

## Severity Definitions
- **Minor** — No injury, minor equipment damage, reversible
- **Moderate** — First aid injury, significant equipment damage
- **Critical** — Serious injury, fatality risk, major equipment loss

## Likelihood Definitions
- **Unlikely** — Has not occurred in similar installations
- **Possible** — Has occurred occasionally in similar installations
- **Likely** — Regularly occurs or expected under normal conditions

## Required Output Format for Risk Assessments
Each finding must state:
1. What the hazard is
2. Severity + Likelihood → Risk level
3. Which standard or requirement it violates
4. Recommended action

## Acceptance Criteria
- CRITICAL: Must be resolved before work proceeds
- HIGH: Must be resolved before project sign-off
- MEDIUM: Action plan required within 30 days
- LOW: Document and monitor
