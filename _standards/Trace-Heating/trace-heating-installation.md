# Trace Heating Installation — 3P Teknik Standard Reference

## Regulatory Basis
- IEC 60335-2-96 (self-regulating heating cables)
- EN 60079-30-1 (trace heating in explosive atmospheres)
- DS/HD 60364-7-753 (Danish: floor heating and trace heating)
- IEC 62395 (resistance heating systems for industrial applications)

## Pre-Installation Checklist
- [ ] Pipe length, diameter, and material confirmed
- [ ] Valve, flange, and meter count documented
- [ ] Required heat output calculated (W/m)
- [ ] Maximum pipe temperature defined
- [ ] ATEX zone assessment completed if applicable

## Installation Requirements
- Fixation: Fibreglass tape every **0.3 m** max
- Non-metallic pipes: Aluminium foil wrap before cable application
- Aluminium foil over cable before thermal insulation
- All connections: Above ground level
- Junction boxes: Drain provision required (bottom drain)
- Thermal insulation: Must exceed max operating temperature

## Electrical Protection
- RCD: Max **30 mA** — mandatory
- Earth: Cable screen/conductor bonded to installation earth
- AFDD recommended for high-risk installations
- Circuit design must specify RCD rating explicitly

## Common Defects to Check
- No RCD specified or rating exceeds 30 mA
- Fibreglass tape spacing exceeds 0.3 m
- Aluminium foil omitted on PVC/FRP pipes
- Junction box has no drain — condensation risk
- Damaged outer sheath — must not be installed
- Standards not referenced in installation documentation
- No thermal insulation material temperature rating stated
